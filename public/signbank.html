"Guess the Word" - Instructions


1. Click on "Get AUSLAN" to view the finger spelling of a word, one letter at a time.
2. Translate the finger spelled letters into English alphabets. If you missed a letter, click "Repeat" to view the finger spelling gestures of the same word again.
3. Enter the translated word into the guessing box and click "Check" to see the result.

N.B. You may use the "interval" to adjust the speed at which images are displayed. To do this, simply drag the interval holder to desired position.

Enjoy!
