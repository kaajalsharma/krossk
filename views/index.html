  A platform created to bridge the communication gaps within our community!


Verbal communication allows individuals to directly express themselves to the wider community. Unfortunately, people with hearing and speaking difficulties tend to remain constrained due to the constant need of visual aids or an interpreter to translate the meaning of their hand gestures. Kross-Konnection has been designed to bridge this communication gap and empower the vulnerable individuals to voice themselves without restrictions. This project presents a translator which converts the Australian Sign Language into literal English alphabets.
